 Parks and Gardens

Uncover Paris: Embracing the City's Lush Parks and Gardens

Luxembourg Gardens

Luxembourg Gardens is a beautiful park featuring manicured lawns, flowers, and fountains, perfect for a leisurely stroll.

Jardin des Serres Auteuil

Jardin des Serres Auteuil is a lovely botanical garden showcasing exotic plants in stunning glass greenhouses.

Tuileries Park

Tuileries Park is a famous public garden located between the Louvre and Place de la Concorde, ideal for relaxing.

Trocadéro Garden

Trocadéro Garden offers fantastic views of the Eiffel Tower, with fountains and sculptures enhancing its beauty.

Champ de Mars

Champ de Mars is a large park located near the Eiffel Tower, perfect for picnics and outdoor activities.

Buttes-Chaumont Park

Buttes-Chaumont Park is a scenic park featuring hills, waterfalls, and a beautiful temple, ideal for leisurely walks.

Square du Vert-Galant

Square du Vert-Galant is a small park at the tip of Île de la Cité, providing lovely views of the Seine.

Ecole Du Breuil

Ecole Du Breuil is a renowned horticultural school, surrounded by beautiful gardens showcasing various plant species.
