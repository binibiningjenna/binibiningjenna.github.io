Discover Paris: Unveiling the City's Iconic Landmarks

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a famous iron tower in Paris, offering stunning views of the city from its top floors.

Arc de Triomphe

The Arc de Triomphe honors those who fought for France, featuring intricate sculptures and an observation deck.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest art museums, housing iconic pieces like the Mona Lisa.

Place des Vosges

Place des Vosges is Paris's oldest planned square, surrounded by beautiful red brick buildings and lush green parks.

Grand Palais

The Grand Palais is an impressive exhibition hall, famous for its stunning glass roof.

Notre-Dame de Paris

Notre-Dame is a historic cathedral known for its stunning Gothic architecture and beautiful stained glass windows.

Montmartre

Montmartre is a charming neighborhood known for its artistic history and cobblestone streets.

River Seine

The River Seine runs through Paris, offering picturesque views and romantic boat cruises along its scenic banks.
